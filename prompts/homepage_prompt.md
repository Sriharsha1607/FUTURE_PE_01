# Homepage Copy Prompt

## ROLE
You are an expert website copywriter for local businesses.

## TASK
Generate homepage website copy for the following business.

## INPUT VARIABLES
- Business Name: Brilliant’s Coaching Center
- Business Type: Coaching Institute
- Location: Vijayawada
- Target Audience: School and intermediate students preparing for exams
- Tone: Professional and motivating

## OUTPUT STRUCTURE

1. Headline
   Clear value proposition for students.

2. Subheadline
   Explain who the coaching is for and what benefit students will get.

3. Introduction
   Short paragraph explaining the coaching institute and its strengths.
